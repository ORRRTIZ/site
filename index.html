About ME

Hello, I'm Ortiz, a 17-year-old from the small town of Cieti, Italy. My creative journey began in 2020 with 3D modeling, but it wasn't until 2022 that I discovered the mesmerizing world of generative art.

I couldn't imagine that with just code alone, one could create such unimaginable works of art that even a brush couldn't reproduce. The incredible art captivated me so much that I decided to dive into it headfirst. For the past two years, I've been studying generative coding, p5.js, Three.js, GLSL, and much more, striving to leave my mark on the digital canvas.

3D-Portfolio

Loops of Life

Once, inspired by another generative project, I decided to create something of my own, something new, something unique. Thus, the idea for the project "Loops Of Life," kinetic sculptures, was born.

Each piece is as unique as every individual's life. Some are vibrant with colors, while others are rendered in black and white tones. Yet, they all share one thing - endless movement, symbolizing the infinity of our lives.

At first glance, this kinetic sculpture utilizes ordinary primitive shapes, but its simplicity only enhances its captivating nature. The absence of distracting elements allows one to relax and detach from everyday worries. Like this small sphere, we often find ourselves teetering on the edge of abysses. In those moments, the key is to keep moving forward and not give up, and then the solution will reveal itself.

Fractal Simphony

In this exploration, I approached the Mandelbrot fractal as if it were a picturesque, infinite landscape embodying the harmony between chaos and order, nature, and mathematics. Delving into the study of the fractal, one can capture striking similarities between the patterns and the world around us. Some may see turbulent river whirlpools in the pattern, some may see lightning bolts in the endless sky, and some may see a blossoming branch of sakura, endowing everyone with its incredible beauty.

Fractal Symphony is an immersion into a virtual space where infinite spirals and captivating patterns become the protagonists of each canvas. Fractal Symphony is a journey into the boundless world of shapes and colors, where the boundaries between mathematics and aesthetics blur. Fractal Symphony is the visualization of the harmony between precise calculations and the chaotic beauty of the universe that surrounds us.
